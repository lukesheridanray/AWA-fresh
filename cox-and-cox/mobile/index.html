BUYERS NOTES

Available in three different sizes, our bestselling Luna rugs are a sumptuous silver shade with a velvety sheen that changes colour in the light.

With a supersoft finish, these short-pile rugs add a touch of luxury to tile or laminate flooring, and are hardwearing and easy to clean.

Our Luna Silver Rug is also available as a runner here.

Made from 100% viscose

To maintain and care for your rug, vacuum regularly to remove dirt and dust

Vacuum or sweep regularly using suction only, a rotating brush can damage the pile surface.

Clean spills immediately. Never rub a spill. Blot liquids with a dry absorbent white cloth or white paper towel. Some stains may be cleaned with a mild clear detergent and lukewarm water.

If a stain cannot be removed seek specialist cleaning advice.

We recommend the use of an anti-slip rug grip when using on wooden, vinyl, laminate or tiled surfaces.
