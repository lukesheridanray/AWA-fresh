Making A Purchase

 
Making a purchase could not be easier. Just browse our online shop, and add any items that you wish to buy into the shopping basket. After you have finished your selection, click on 'Checkout' and you will be asked for a few details that we will need in order to be able to complete the order. We accept credit and debit card payment. If you are shopping from outside the UK, place your order and your credit card company will convert the transaction cost into your own currency. When confirmation of your order is received, this is to indicate that we have received your order. It does not indicate that a contract exists between us. We will indicate acceptance of your order, and hence a contract between us, when we send you an invoice. We have included this term to protect us in the case that a mistake has been made in pricing, we have inadvertently under-priced goods, or we are no longer able to supply a particular product for some reason. In the case of a change of price, we will always contact you first to ensure that the price is acceptable.
 

Shipping And Handling

 
From 12th September 2011, our standard UK P&P is £3.95. Delivery is FREE on orders over £40 (excluding delivery)  being sent to UK addresses. We also offer an Express dispatch service for a charge of £5.95. This charge is instead of the £3.95 standard charge, but is applicable on Express orders of all sizes and values. Orders being sent to addresses outside the UK will have a charge added for postage/courier, which is calculated for you online.
 
Standard delivery times are 7 to 10 working days for personal orders (although this is usually much quicker). For school orders please allow 21 days for delivery. Express orders are dispatched within 2 working days of receipt of your order and are sent either 1st class Royal Mail or with a guaranteed courier service. International delivery times will vary. For all orders please allow a maximum of 28 days for delivery. Please note that smaller UK orders are currently sent by 2nd class Royal Mail. Larger UK deliveries are sent by DHL or Yodel 48 hr delivery. Please note that Map Jigsaws are sent from a separate supplier and can take up to 21 days for delivery.
 

Back Orders

 
If your item is not in stock, we will back order it for you. You will always be emailed with the option to cancel your order if you would rather not wait. Please note that you will not be charged for items until they are ready for dispatch.
 

Credit Card Security

 
When your order is placed on our website, credit card numbers are encrypted using 128 bit encryption. They are not held in clear text on our web site.

Privacy Policy

 
Cookies are used on this shopping site to keep track of the contents of your shopping basket, to store delivery addresses if the address book is used and to store your details if you select the 'Remember Me' Option. They are also used after you have logged on as part of that process. You can turn off cookies within your browser by going to 'Tools | Internet Options | Privacy' and selecting to block cookies. If you turn off cookies, you will be unable to place orders or benefit from the other features that use cookies.
 

Returns Policy

 
Unless otherwise stated, we offer a full money back guarantee if goods are returned within fourteen days of receipt. This applies to UK orders only. We regret that goods returned used, damaged, opened or in damaged packaging cannot be refunded. The description of all products is clear on the box and there should be no need to remove protective packaging, such as cellophane wraps, which are in place to ensure products we supply to children are not subjected to tampering and hence removal will mean products cannot safely be resold. If in doubt, please call our Customer Services team. We regret that unless the goods are faulty, we are unable to refund postage and packaging costs. Please note that returns are entirely the customer's responsibility.

We strongly suggest that you insure against loss. We regret that we cannot be held responsible for returned goods that are damaged en route, or that do not arrive back. Postage will only be refunded in the case of damaged or faulty goods. Please note that any errors or omissions from an order must be reported within seven days of receipt of the items. Your rights to return goods are protected under the EU Distance Selling Directive which can be found at http://www.hmso.gov.uk/si/si2000/20002334.htm
