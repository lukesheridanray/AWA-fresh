We guarantee that this product is cheaper than on any other website. If this is found not to be the case - even after your purchase - we'll refund the difference, plus an extra 10%.

How do I claim?

Place your order as normal. If you become aware of a cheaper price on a different website within 7 days of receiving it, contact us for a refund.
Just email info@northernparrots.com with the following details

  • The order number and product code(s) from your invoice
  • The website where you've seen the product - please include a link to the exact page
  • The price you've seen, including delivery

Once we've verified it we'll issue a refund, by the same method you paid, within 7 days. It's that simple!


Example

Our total price is £49.99
You've seen the same product at £44.99 in total.
We'll refund the difference of £5 plus an extra 10% making a total refund of £5.50.


The Small Print
